Within schooling, I work for the organization Women in Sciences and Computing. The organization aims to increase diversity in STEM and foster an encouraging environment on campus.

Product Design

As a side hobby, I create graphics to sell on e-commerce sites such as Redbubble. I am currently selling tech-themed streetwear! I love to work on apparel and stickers.

Web Development

As the Director of Graphic Design for WiSC, I am responsible for designing and building our website in collaboration with the Director of Marketing. I design high-fidelity wireframes in Figma and customize the site using a combination of HTML and CSS.

One of my studies at UofT focuses on GIS - Geographic Information Systems - and to further my understanding, I took part in the ESRI student associates program. I write blog entries on new and current GIS technologies being developed.

UX/UI
